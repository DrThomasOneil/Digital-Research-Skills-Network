Flow Cytometry in R: Tools, Techniques, and Transitions

This interactive session will dive into the analytical considerations for flow cytometry, covering key factors like batch effects, transformations, compensation, and QC. We’ll explore software and R-based packages, with presentations on FuseSOM and Spectre, providing practical insights for researchers looking to expand their flow cytometry analysis beyond traditional software.

Time: Tuesday 15th April, 1-3 pm

In person: WIMR Seminar Room

Online: Zoom Link

Speakers:

  • Givanna Putri & Felix Marsh-Wakefield on Spectre
  • Elijah Willie on FuseSOM
