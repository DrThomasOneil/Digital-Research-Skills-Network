PrecisionGo Presents…

Getting started with Spatial Transcriptomics at WIMR

DRSN will be collaborating with PrecisionGo and 10X Genomics to explore the options at WIMR for Spatial Transcriptomics, and the key considerations when analyzing Visum and Xenium data. Join us for the informative Seminar session for how you can get started and what to expect from Spatial Transcriptomics. Stick around for the In-focus discussion where we’ll be joined by Geoffrey McDermott (10X Genomics) to discuss Xenium and Visium data preparations (e.g. panel design, cell segmentation) and data analysis. Lastly, join Thomas O’Neil at the Analysis Workshop to learn how you can download public Visium data, processs it and start creating visualisations yourself, immediately elevating your thesis, manuscript or grant application.
