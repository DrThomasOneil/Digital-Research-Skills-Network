Bring your own data!

Tired of example data that never quite fits your needs? You’re in luck!

This month, we’re hosting a Bring Your Own Data session - a hands-on, self-guided workshop designed to help you get started with analysing your own dataset.

Each session will begin with a brief presentation showcasing the core tools and workflows we typically use in R. After a short break, you’ll have the chance to apply those tools to your own data. Our team will be available throughout to guide, troubleshoot, and help you move forward with your analysis - no matter the dataset or research question.

(lunch from 12pm)
